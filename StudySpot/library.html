Ithaca College - Library

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850



The library is full of helpful resources for whatever project you may be doing. Depending on the floor you are on will decide how loud the floor may be. The library allows for a more social floor on the first floor where students can print off assignments discuss projects with group members and the librarians and visit with friends. As students make their ways up to the 5th floor the study spots become more and more silent.
