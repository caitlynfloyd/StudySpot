Coffee Shop Study Spots






Starbucks

130 E Seneca St, Ithaca, NY 14850

Starbucks is a classic spot for coffee lovers. It is normally very busy and noisy due to the employees making coffee.






Chatty Cathy Coffee and Juice Co.

327 Eddy St, Ithaca, NY 14850

Chatty Cathy is a quiet coffee shop located in downtown Cornell. Enjoy a nice acai bowl while you crack down on work.






Gimme! Coffee

506 W. State St, Ithaca, NY 14850

Gimme! Coffee is a nice place to work with a buddy or just relax and have a coffee. The coffee shop is built nicely for students.
