Small Study Spots






Textor Hall

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

Textor Hall is a great place to study at night. The halls are silent with lots of comfortable places to sit.






Friends Tech Lab

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

The Friends Tech Lab is a great place to study and print out with during the day and is open 24/7 for students.






Tompkins County Library

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

The Tompkins County Library is the perfect spot if students want to study in a library setting, but also would like to get off campus.
