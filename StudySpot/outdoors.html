Outdoor Areas






Ithaca College - Chapel

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

The Chapel is a quiet and peaceful place to study or take a break. The chapel is generally not very crowded and is a great place to get away when students need to during busy school weeks.






Ithaca College - Fountains

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

The fountains are very relaxing to study or relax next to at any time during the day especially when it is warm outside.






Robert H. Treman State Park

105 Enfield Falls Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

Treman Park is a good place to go to if you need to escape the stress of campus life and school work. Come here to enjoy nature with friends or read a book by the falls.






Textor Hall

953 Danby Rd, Ithaca, NY 14850

Textor Hall is a great place to study at night. The halls are silent with lots of comfortable places to sit.
